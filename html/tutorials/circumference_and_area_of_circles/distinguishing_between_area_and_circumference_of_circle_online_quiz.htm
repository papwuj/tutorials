Q 1 - James wants to buy cookies for his friend. The radius of a cookie is 3.6 inches. What is the cookieâ€™s circumference?

A - 20.61 in

B - 22.61 in

C - 24.61 in

D - 27.61 in

Answer : B

Explanation

Step 1:

Given radius r = 3.6 in

Step 2:

Circumference of the Cookie = 2πr = 2(3.14)3.6 = 22.61 in

Q 2 - Charles wants to put a circular table in middle of his room. The diameter of the table is 4.8 meters. Calculate how much area is covered by the table?

A - 12.09 square meters

B - 15.09 square meters

C - 16.09 square meters

D - 18.09 square meters

Answer : D

Explanation

Step 1:

Given diameter = 4.8 m ; radius r = 4.8/2 = 2.4 m

Step 2:

Area covered by table = πr2 = (3.14)(2.4)(2.4) = 18.09 square meters

Q 9 - A Ferris wheel has a diameter of 210 feet. How many feet will the Ferris wheel rotate in one complete turn?

A - 659.4 ft

D - 699.4 ft

Explanation

Q 10 - The circumference of a circle is 16.52 feet. What is the approximate length of the radius?

A - 2.13 ft

B - 2.33 ft

C - 2.63 ft

D - 2.69 ft

Answer : C

Explanation

Step 1:

Circumference of Circle = 2πr = 2(3.14)r = 16.52 ft

Step 2:

Radius r = 16.52/(2 × 3.14) = 2.63 ft
