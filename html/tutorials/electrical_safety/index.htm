Electrical Safety Tutorial

In the field of electrical engineering, engineers and other professionals are exposed to electricity indirectly during generation, transportation, installation and usage. Such conditions might cause hazards if accurate safety measures are not taken. This tutorial will help you understand measures that can be taken for electrical safety.

Audience

This tutorial has been prepared for electrical engineers and people aspiring to make electrical engineering their profession.

Prerequisites

We assume that you have preliminary level of knowledge on operation of basic electrical devices and machines.
